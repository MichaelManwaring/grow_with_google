  • Scientific Name: Phycodurus eques
  • Average Length: 20-24 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 7 years
  • Habitat: Southern Australian seagrass

The Leafy Seadragon is a ridgid-bodied relative of the seahorse family that hides among seagrass barely moving. Its namesake "leafy" protrusions are immobile and its propulsion is provided by a translucent fin on its back.
